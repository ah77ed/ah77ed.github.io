About

ATHAR — Architectural Design Studio.

We work on residential, commercial, and interior projects with one methodology: structure precedes form. This archive documents every project with precision, from the first idea to the construction details.

Scope of Work

01

Residential Design

Villas and apartments, from the initial layout to precise construction details.

02

Interior Design

Interior spaces that align with the structural logic of the building as a whole.

03

Design Consulting

Accompanying the project from the first idea through to construction-ready documentation.

02

Principles

01

Structure First

Every space is designed around its structural logic before any formal decision.

02

Material As-Is

Concrete and brick are left exposed, not hidden behind excessive finishes.

03

Construction Precision

A strong design has to be genuinely buildable, within a realistic budget.
